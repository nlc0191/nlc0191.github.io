Biography

Yekwon Park was born in Seoul, South Korea on November 21st of the year 1997. He moved to the United States at the age of 9 on October 9th, 2006. Since then, he has grown up in Irvine, California, finishing up his high school career at Arnold O. Beckman High School. Currently, he is a sophomore at Tufts University, studying Computer Science in the College of Engineering.
